What this hub is for

Audit concepts translated into practical business actions.

This hub is designed to help business owners, small teams, and professionals understand what can go wrong in a process, why it matters, what control should exist, what evidence should be kept, and how to implement the control in a simple way.

01

Understand the risk

Identify what can go wrong in a business process.

02

Build the control

Define practical checks, approvals, reviews, or routines.

03

Keep the evidence

Know what documentation should exist to prove the control happened.
